
# Introduction to Cybersecurity 🔐

## What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, or damage.

It involves tools, processes, and strategies used to secure information and ensure privacy.

---

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?

- Protects sensitive data (personal, financial, business)
- Prevents cyber attacks and data breaches
- Ensures system reliability and availability
- Builds trust in digital systems

---

## Benefits of Cybersecurity

- 🛡️ Data protection
- 🔒 Privacy and confidentiality
- 💼 Career opportunities (high-demand field)
- 🌍 Supports safe digital transformation

---

## Negative Aspects / Challenges

- ❗ Constantly evolving threats
- 💰 Can be expensive to implement
- 🧠 Requires continuous learning
- ⚠️ Human errors can still cause breaches
